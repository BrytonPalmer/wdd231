About Us

Our Mission

Clean Sweep is a proud family business founded by a mother and her daughters. From the very beginning, we've kept it in the family to ensure quality and care every time we visit your home. We genuinely love helping lighten your load—even in the simplest ways. We believe a clean home fosters a happier, healthier life for you and your loved ones. When you choose Clean Sweep, you're not just hiring a service—you're welcoming a team of caring, hardworking women who treat your home like their own. Let us help you reclaim your space and peace of mind. After all, who better to lend a hand than mothers who understand the value of a well-kept home?
